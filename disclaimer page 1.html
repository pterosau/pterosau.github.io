Response - Prototype Disclaimer

Response is currently a prototype platform, developed to demonstrate proof of concept and showcase the potential of our social media vision. Due to the current limitations in our development process and the inability to fully clone certain features from more mature platforms, Response is being presented "as-is." We aim to attract early users and gather valuable feedback to refine and improve the platform over time. Please note that some features may be incomplete or subject to change as we continue to evolve and enhance the user experience.
